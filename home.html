🌍 About This Journal

Every day, thousands of research papers are published — brilliant work, often buried in technical language. Open Science takes those studies and retells them clearly, connecting the newest discoveries to real-world meaning, historical context and practical applications to a broader audience of curious and avid readers.

Our audience:

  • 🧩 Curious readers building their general knowledge
  • ⚙️ Engineers improving their craft
  • ☕ Researchers reading with morning coffee

Here, science isn’t just reported; it’s interpreted.
